Directory and Authority Services

National Archives and Records Administration

Welcome to the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) Description and Authority Services. The Description and Authority services were created to help manage and organize NARA’s nationwide holdings in the Washington, DC area, Regional Archives and Presidential Libraries. The Description Service allows for the management of existing descriptions of archival material already published and available to the public. Additionally, the Description Service provides Describers a way to create new descriptions based upon new and existing holdings and send those descriptions through an approval workflow. The Authority Service allows cataloguers to manage the authorities that index the descriptions and provide consistent retrieval of archival materials.
